Simple Dice Roller

Command looks like: <command> <dice1> <dice2> ... <bonus>
Where <command> = [ min, max, sum, roll, ability ]; <dice> = <count>d<sides>
min: finds the minimum among all dice rolls and adds bonus
max: finds the maximum among all dice rolls and adds bonus
sum: finds the sum among all dice rolls and adds bonus
roll: shows the first four dice rolls
ability: counts one of the ability scores when generating a character

Examples: roll 2d4 | sum 2d6 1d4 2 | min 2d20 -3 | ability | sum 2d6 d8 3d10
